=== 1. STANDARD MCQ ===
Q1. Which of the following is the main objective of teaching?
(A) To give information related to the syllabus.
(B) To develop thinking power and critical analytical ability in students.
(C) To dictate notes to students in classroom sessions.
(D) To prepare students to pass the examination with high percentages.
Answer: B

=== 2. ASSERTION & REASONING ===
Q2. Assertion (A): Format and styles of referencing in a thesis are standardized across academic disciplines.
Reason (R): Standardization ensures uniformity, clarity, and ease of cross-referencing for researchers.
(A) Both (A) and (R) are true and (R) is the correct explanation of (A)
(B) Both (A) and (R) are true but (R) is NOT the correct explanation of (A)
(C) (A) is true but (R) is false
(D) (A) is false but (R) is true
Answer: A
